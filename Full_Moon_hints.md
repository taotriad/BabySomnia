# Full Moon: Hints and details to look out for

Full Moon is the first fan song released by Dreamcatcher.
It was released on 2018-01-12 to commemorate Dreamcatcher's first anniversary.
The release was preceded by a Reveal Countdown live,
where they played short snippets of the song and talked about it.

At first only a promotional music video was released.
The promotional music video ended with a very special surprise
(the date of Dreamcatcher's next comeback).

A month later a full-length official choreography music video was released.

## Notes

* Full Moon was originally only released as digital single, but it was later re-released on Dreamcatcher's first full-length album, Dystopia: The Tree of Language.
* The members of Dreamcatcher had not been notified in advance when their next comeback would be, so when they watched the MV during their live broadcast
  they were quite shocked and screamed of excitement.
* The first live performance of Full Moon was on Dreamcatcher's first anniversary fan meeting (2018-01-13).
* Lee Ja Eun was in attendance at the fan meeting.
